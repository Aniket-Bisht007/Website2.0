About Vikalpa Foundation

Vikalpa Foundation is dedicated to creating meaningful and sustainable social impact by empowering individuals and communities through education, skill development, mentorship, and innovative programs. We believe that every individual deserves equal opportunities to learn, grow, and contribute to society regardless of their background or circumstances. Our initiatives focus on nurturing talent, strengthening educational outcomes, promoting social awareness, and building pathways for long-term development. Through collaborative efforts with educational institutions, government bodies, community leaders, and volunteers, we strive to bridge gaps in access to quality resources and opportunities. At Vikalpa Foundation, we envision a future where young minds are equipped with the knowledge, confidence, and support necessary to achieve their aspirations. By fostering innovation, inclusivity, and community participation, we aim to create lasting change that positively impacts generations to come. Our commitment remains rooted in transparency, accountability, and a passion for transforming lives through collective action and purposeful engagement.

Transforming Education

Our Flagship Programs

Driving educational excellence and foundational learning through impactful initiatives.

Haryana Super 100

Haryana Super 100 is a fully funded residential initiative focused on preparing talented government school students for competitive examinations such as IIT-JEE and NEET. Through expert mentorship, academic support, and structured learning environments, the program empowers students to achieve excellence and unlock greater educational opportunities.

Mission Buniyaad

Mission Buniyaad aims to strengthen foundational literacy and numeracy skills among young learners. By focusing on core educational outcomes, the program helps students build a strong academic base, enhancing their confidence, learning capabilities, and long-term educational success.
