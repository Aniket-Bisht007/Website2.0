About The Program

Haryana Super 100

Haryana Super 100 is a flagship initiative dedicated to identifying and nurturing talented students from government schools and preparing them for highly competitive examinations such as IIT-JEE, NEET, and other national-level entrance tests. Through expert faculty, structured mentorship, quality study material, residential coaching, regular assessments, and focused academic support, the program empowers students to unlock their highest potential and achieve excellence. Haryana Super 100 has helped hundreds of students secure admissions into prestigious institutions, creating opportunities for brighter futures and transforming lives through quality education.
